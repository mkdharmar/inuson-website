Market Context

Why IT talent is genuinely hard to hire

The technology talent market remains one of the most competitive in the U.S. economy. Demand from non-tech employers — financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, energy, and government — has absorbed a significant portion of available technical talent. Engineers and developers who were previously exclusive to tech companies are now fielding approaches from organizations in every sector.

For most technical roles above the entry level, the strongest candidates are not actively applying to job postings. They are employed, performing well, and open only to the right conversation. Reaching them requires a recruiter with the technical credibility to have that conversation — someone who understands the stack, the architecture patterns, and what makes the opportunity genuinely interesting.

Inuson brings both the network and the technical literacy to source effectively in this market. We reach professionals others cannot, evaluate them rigorously, and present only those we are prepared to advocate for. Read our IT Staffing Guide →
