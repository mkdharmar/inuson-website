Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Inuson International Inc. ("Inuson," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who visit our website and interact with our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, company, email address, phone number, when you submit a form or contact us directly.
  • Professional information — job title, hiring needs, resume content, when provided voluntarily.
  • Usage data — IP address, browser type, pages visited, collected automatically via standard web server logs.

How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide staffing and recruitment services.
  • Match qualified candidates with relevant opportunities.
  • Communicate with you about your request or engagement.
  • Improve our website and service quality.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, subject to confidentiality obligations.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.
