About Inuson

General questions about Inuson International Inc.

Inuson International Inc. is a U.S.-based staffing and recruitment company that connects organizations with exceptional talent through Contract Staffing, Direct Hire, Executive Search, and Recruitment Process Support.

Inuson International Inc. has been in business for over 10 years, with a team that brings more than 20 years of combined industry and recruiting experience.

Inuson International Inc. is based at 315 Lowell Avenue, Hamilton, NJ 08619, United States. We serve organizations nationwide throughout the United States.

Inuson is relationship-driven and quality-focused. We build long-term partnerships rather than transactional placements, combining recruiting expertise with deep market knowledge.

Services

Questions about Inuson's staffing and recruitment services.

Inuson offers four core service lines: Contract Staffing (temporary, project-based, and long-term), Direct Hire (permanent placement), Executive Search (senior and specialized professionals), and Recruitment Process Support (augmenting internal talent acquisition teams).

Contract staffing places qualified professionals with your organization for a defined period, contributing the skills your projects require without a permanent employment commitment.

Direct hire places professionals directly into your organization as permanent employees, with Inuson managing sourcing, screening, and placement from start to finish.

Executive search is a targeted, research-driven approach to identifying senior leaders and specialized professionals — including those not actively seeking new opportunities.

Recruitment Process Support augments your internal talent acquisition team with external recruiting capacity, adding qualified resources on a flexible, scalable basis.

Timelines depend on role complexity, location, and market availability. Inuson works from an active, pre-screened network to move quickly while maintaining quality.
