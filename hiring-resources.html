Hiring Resources for Employers & Candidates

Practical guides, tools, and insights to help employers build better hiring processes — and candidates advance their careers.

For Employers

Hiring Guides

Resources to help you hire more effectively and work with a staffing partner.

Write an Effective Job Description

A compelling job description attracts stronger candidates. Include responsibilities, required skills, compensation range, and growth opportunity. Avoid jargon and unrealistic requirement lists.

Choose the Right Staffing Model

Contract staffing provides flexibility for project-based needs. Direct hire is ideal for permanent roles. Executive search targets senior leaders. Match the model to your goal for faster, better outcomes.

Run a Structured Interview Process

Define competencies and scoring criteria before interviews begin. Move quickly — top candidates are typically off the market within two weeks. Assign clear roles to each interviewer and consolidate feedback promptly.

Benchmark Compensation Competitively

Competitive pay is the single most effective hiring tool. Research rates by role, experience level, and geography. Include total compensation context — benefits, flexibility, and growth — in every offer conversation.

For Candidates

Career Guides

Tools and advice to help you present yourself effectively and negotiate with confidence.

Resume Tips from Recruiters

Use a clean format. Lead with a strong summary. Quantify achievements wherever possible. Tailor your resume to each role using keywords from the job description. Keep it to one or two pages and proofread carefully.

Interview Preparation Checklist

Research the company before every interview. Prepare STAR-method examples for each key requirement. Arrive prepared with thoughtful questions for the interviewer. Send a thank-you note within 24 hours.

Salary Negotiation Strategies

Know your target range before the conversation. Let the employer make the first offer when possible. Anchor high within a realistic range and support your ask with market data and your specific track record.

Conduct a Confidential Job Search

Working with a recruiter lets you explore opportunities without alerting your employer. We present only roles that match your criteria and never share your information without your explicit consent.
